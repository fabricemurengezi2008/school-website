About Us

Shaping Rwanda's Future, One Student at a Time

CPEC SAINT BABETH is a private educational institution located in Byumba, Northern Province, Rwanda. Our mission is to provide affordable, quality education that empowers students with both academic knowledge and practical technical skills.

We believe every child deserves a chance to succeed. Our dedicated teachers, modern technical workshops, and nurturing environment make CPEC Saint Babeth the ideal place for your child's educational journey.
